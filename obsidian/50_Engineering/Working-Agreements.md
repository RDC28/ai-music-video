# Working Agreements

## Delivery Standards
- Every non-trivial change links to a task.
- Architecture-affecting changes link to an ADR.
- Major proposals start as RFCs before implementation.

## Reliability Standards
- Incidents get an incident note + postmortem.
- Runbooks are updated within 24h of repeated operational issues.

## Communication Standards
- Keep status updates concise and timestamped.
- Record tradeoffs, not just final decisions.
